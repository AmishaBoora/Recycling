ELECTRONIC WASTE RECYCLING
"Help the earth, recycle your tech"
E-waste recycling is the process of extracting valuable materials after shredding the e-waste into tiny pieces that could be reused in a new electronic appliance. But a number of current challenges are preventing the electronic recycling industry from scaling up. We explore how e-waste recycling is done and why we should focus on scaling it up.
